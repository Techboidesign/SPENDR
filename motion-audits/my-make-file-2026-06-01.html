CriticalJakubEmil

No app-wide prefers-reduced-motion fallback

WhatGrep shows zero reduced-motion rules under src/. Five files use useReducedMotion() from Motion; Home, Insights, globals chart CSS, BottomSheetModal, FabExpenseLauncher, and BottomTabBar do not.

Why it mattersUsers with vestibular sensitivity get full 700ms mount fades and spring tab highlights with no OS-level escape hatch.

Recommended motionAdd @media (prefers-reduced-motion: reduce) in globals.css (collapse durations) and a small useAppMotion() hook that wraps Motion + documents when inline animation: styles should be omitted.
